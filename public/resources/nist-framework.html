NIST Cybersecurity Framework Guide

Executive Summary

The NIST Cybersecurity Framework provides a policy framework for organizations to assess and improve their cybersecurity posture.

Five Core Functions:

  • IDENTIFY: Develop organizational understanding to manage cybersecurity risk
  • PROTECT: Implement safeguards to ensure delivery of critical services
  • DETECT: Develop activities to identify cybersecurity events
  • RESPOND: Take action regarding detected cybersecurity incidents
  • RECOVER: Maintain resilience and restore capabilities

Implementation Tiers

Tier 1: Partial - Ad hoc risk management

Tier 2: Risk Informed - Management approved practices

Tier 3: Repeatable - Formal policies and procedures

Tier 4: Adaptive - Lessons learned integration

Framework Profiles

Current Profile: Current state of cybersecurity activities

Target Profile: Desired cybersecurity outcomes

Gap Analysis: Comparison to identify improvements
